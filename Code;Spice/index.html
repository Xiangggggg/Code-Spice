Why join the Code;Spice program?

The Code;Spice program teaches people about beginner and intermediate level of programming, including Python, which is fun, useful, and easy to learn.

What is the cost of attending?

Code;Spice is completely free! Also, there isn't a huge time commitment necessary to this program. Our schedules are built to accomodate yours!

What can people expect from the program?

People can expect immersive, interactive, and instructive lessons. They will be able to master programming concepts and confidently build Python applications by the end of the course.
