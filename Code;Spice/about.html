More About Code;Spice

Code;Spice is a coding camp completely free of charge for kids of all ages that would like to learn Python, relatively simple algorithmic thinking, how to get started on your own project, or just the coding philosophy in general. From August to September, we will be meeting twice a week for 1.5-2 hours, in addition to optional office hours to help students with questions or problems. Recordings of classes will be posted for participants that missed classes. We hope for participants to not only learn how to code, but also how to think like a coder, code more efficiently, and most importantly, have fun!
