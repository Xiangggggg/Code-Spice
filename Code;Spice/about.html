More About Code;Spice

Code;Spice is a coding camp completely free of charge for kids of all ages that would like to learn Python, relatively simple algorithmic thinking, how to get started on your own project, or just the coding philosophy in general. The camp is 69420 weeks long with meetings every day. We hope to not only teach just how to code, but also how to think like a coder, code more efficiently, and most importantly, have fun!
